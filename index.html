"There is a very strong correlation between childhood engagement in the creative arts and measurable success later in life" - Michigan State University

Zhong Art Academy is a local private Ottawa art school that provides art classes to aspiring students of any level. We focus primarily on teaching drawing and painting skills, but we are flexible!

Drawing And Sketching

Zhong Art Academy curates professional, realistic drawing skills for students at any level. Learn from drawing simple shapes to sketching ultra-realistic human portraits, famous landmarks, comtemporary scenary, and even animals. The sky's limit to what you can draw

Gallery

See works of art by our students and pictures of our facility

Painting

For more advanced learners, we also offer classes on painting. Learn how to paint everything from your favorite animals, classical natural landscapes, to symbolic contemporary artwork

Art Competitions

Zhong Art Academy has had a very successful record with numerous artistic competitions. Each and every student is actively encouraged to participate in regional and school-based artistic competitions. Every year dozens of our students submit beautiful pieces of art to regional competitions such as Young at Art and bring back numerous awards. For every art competition, students bring art pieces that they have worked on to our school where they can spend time working on it and to receive professional advice.
